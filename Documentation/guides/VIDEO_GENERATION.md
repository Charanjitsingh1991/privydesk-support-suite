# PrivyDesk Homepage Slider Video - Generation Prompt

## Video Concept
Create a modern, professional 15-20 second promotional video showcasing PrivyDesk as an AI-powered customer support platform.

## Video Specifications
- **Duration**: 15-20 seconds
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 (1920x1080)
- **Style**: Modern, clean, tech-forward with smooth animations
- **Color Scheme**: Black background with lime green (#a3e635) accents
- **Mood**: Professional, innovative, efficient, trustworthy

## Scene Breakdown

### Scene 1 (0-5 seconds): The Problem
**Visual**: Show a frustrated business owner surrounded by:
- Multiple chat windows piling up
- Overflowing email inbox notifications
- Social media messages flooding in
- Support tickets scattered everywhere
- Clock ticking fast showing time pressure

**Text Overlay**: "Drowning in customer support requests?"

**Voiceover/Caption**: "Managing customer support across multiple channels is overwhelming"

### Scene 2 (5-10 seconds): The Solution Introduction
**Visual**: 
- Screen transitions to clean, organized PrivyDesk dashboard
- Show the PrivyDesk logo with lime green accent
- Unified inbox consolidating all channels (email, chat, social)
- Smooth animation of tickets being automatically organized

**Text Overlay**: "Meet PrivyDesk - Your AI-Powered Support Hub"

**Voiceover/Caption**: "PrivyDesk unifies all your support channels in one intelligent platform"

### Scene 3 (10-15 seconds): Key Features Showcase
**Visual**: Quick cuts showing:
1. **AI Automation**: Robot icon auto-routing tickets with smart suggestions
2. **Multi-Channel**: Icons of email, chat, WhatsApp, social media merging into one
3. **Real-Time Analytics**: Dashboard with live graphs and metrics
4. **Team Collaboration**: Multiple user avatars working together seamlessly
5. **Fast Response**: Lightning bolt icon with response time counter

**Text Overlay** (appearing one by one):
- "AI-Powered Automation"
- "Omnichannel Support"
- "Real-Time Analytics"
- "Team Collaboration"
- "Lightning Fast"

### Scene 4 (15-20 seconds): The Result & CTA
**Visual**:
- Happy customers giving thumbs up/5-star ratings
- Support team relaxed and efficient
- Growing business metrics (upward arrows, positive graphs)
- PrivyDesk logo with tagline

**Text Overlay**: 
"Transform Your Customer Support Today"
"Start Free - No Credit Card Required"

**Voiceover/Caption**: "Join thousands of businesses delivering exceptional support with PrivyDesk"
